About the Journal

Aims and Scope

The East African Journal of Neurological Sciences (EAJNS) is dedicated to advancing the field of neurological sciences through the dissemination of high-quality research and innovative findings. As the official journal of the East African Association of Neurological Surgeons (EAANS), EAJNS aims to provide a platform for researchers, clinicians, and practitioners to share knowledge and contribute to the development of neurosurgery and related disciplines in Africa and beyond.

Mission Statement

The East African Journal of Neurological Sciences (EAJNS) is dedicated to advancing the field of neurological sciences by publishing rigorous, peer-reviewed research. Our mission is to foster regional and global collaboration, drive innovation in the understanding and management of neurological disorders, and provide medical professionals and researchers with a trusted platform for the dissemination of critical knowledge and scientific advancements.
